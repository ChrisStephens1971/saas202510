### What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't:**
- Rotate the logo
- Stretch or squish (always maintain aspect ratio)
- Change colors (except approved variations)
- Add effects (drop shadows, glows, etc.)
- Outline the logo
- Place on busy backgrounds without container
- Recreate or redraw the logo
- Change any logo elements
- Add your own text to the logo

✅ **Do:**
- Scale proportionally
- Use approved color variations
- Maintain clear space
- Use high-resolution files
- Place on appropriate backgrounds
- Use latest version
